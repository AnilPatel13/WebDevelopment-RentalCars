Welcome to WhyGo

A subscription-based model for rental cars is an innovative and convenient way for people to access vehicles without the long-term commitment of buying or leasing. This model allows customers to pay a monthly fee for access to a vehicle or a fleet of vehicles, and the rental company takes care of all the maintenance, insurance, and other associated costs. Here are some benefits of subscribing to a rental car service:

  • Flexibility to switch vehicle as often as you like.
  • No long-term commitment
  • Cost-effective - don't have to worry about the upfront cost of buying a vehicle
  • Maintenance and repairs/Customization
  • Insurance
